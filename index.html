About Michael

Dedicated to Finding Your Perfect Home

Whether you are a first time home buyer or seeking to purchase a profitable investment property, the David Lyng team will surpass your expectations with its customized, client driven services that are designed to fit your individual needs.

My experience in the real estate industry enables me to analyze various residential and commercial transactions with a systematic and comprehensive business strategy. I pride myself in being conscientious and knowledgeable about the Santa Cruz County neighborhoods and surrounding areas.

Listening to my client is an important part of the process, whether you are buying or selling property. Understanding your needs, budget and time frame allows me to provide you with a listing of properties that best fits your requirements. I will utilize the latest technologies, market research and business strategies to exceed your expectations and find the home that matches all of your criteria.

Building relationships with our clients is what we do best at David Lyng Real Estate. I will strive to provide you with the highest level of individualized service. I hope your experience with me will be something that you will remember, enjoy and share with others. I look forward to hearing from you. Please email/call and we can begin planning your future.
